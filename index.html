Welcome to CCM!


Catholic Campus Ministry brings the message of the Gospel and the Catholic faith to the TCNJ community. We provide a variety of religious programs, services, and activities to meet TCNJ students' spiritual needs.
CCM offers weekend Mass on campus at TCNJ's Spiritual Center, located across from Norsworthy Hall on D Street. The ministry has the full-time services of a priest/chaplain, supported through the Diocese of Trenton.
Check out below to learn more about what we do!


What's CCM up to?


Service Projects

We host service projects to benefit our local community. Our common projects include making PB&Js for distribution, assembling Thanksgiving and Easter baskets, and coloring print-outs for children in hospitals.

Discussion Group

Living Our Faith Together (LOFT) is our weekly discussion group meeting where we talk about different faith-based topics. Our meetings are a great way to find community within our campus. All are welcome!

Retreats

We host retreats as a space for our students to connect outside of Mass. We take part in social games, lunch, Mass, prayer, the rosary, and Adoration.
